Shipping Policy

Overview

Alburnum creates handcrafted items made from wood, metal, and paper. Because our products vary in size, shape, and materials, shipping costs are not always predictable at checkout.

How shipping is handled

  • Shipping is not automatically calculated for all items
  • Shipping is calculated after the order is placed
  • You will receive a shipping quote before the item ships

No order ships without shipping approval.

Why shipping varies

  • Some items are lightweight but oversized
  • Some items require custom packaging
  • Larger items ship more accurately by weight

After you place an order

  1. You place the order
  2. We review size, weight, and destination
  3. We calculate actual shipping cost
  4. We contact you with the shipping total
  5. You approve the shipping charge
  6. Your order ships

Request a shipping quote before ordering

If you want a shipping estimate before purchasing, contact us and include the product name and your ZIP code.

Local pickup

Local pickup may be available depending on the item. This will be discussed during order confirmation.
